Explore Taniti

Taniti is a tropical paradise with something for everyone. From sandy beaches and lush rainforests to a vibrant local culture, our island offers a unique experience for all visitors.

Featured Attractions

Active Volcano

Experience the thrill of visiting our active volcano. Guided tours are available daily.

Lush Rainforests

Explore the diverse flora and fauna of Taniti's rainforests. Perfect for hiking and zip-lining.

Pristine Beaches

Relax on our sandy beaches and enjoy the crystal-clear waters of Yellow Leaf Bay.
