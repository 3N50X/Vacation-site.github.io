Explore Taniti

Taniti is a tropical paradise with something for everyone. From sandy beaches and lush rainforests to a vibrant local culture, our island offers a unique experience for all visitors.

Resaurants

Taniti boasts 10 diverse dining options: five featuring local fish and rice, three offering American-style meals, and two specializing in Pan-Asian cuisine.

Grocery Stores

Taniti has two supermarkets, two smaller grocery stores, and one convenience store that is open 24 hours a day.

Lodging

From an affordable hostel to a luxurious four-star resort, Taniti offers a variety of lodging options, including family-owned hotels and bed and breakfasts. All accommodations are regulated and inspected by the government.

Entertainment

Visitors enjoy beaches, rainforests, and volcano tours. Additional activities include a local history museum, chartered fishing tours, snorkeling, rainforest zip-lining, pubs (including a microbrewery), a dance club, a movie theater, helicopter rides, an arcade, art galleries, and bowling. A nine-hole golf course will open next year. Many attractions are in Merriton Landing, a rapidly developing area on Yellow Leaf Bay's north side.

Pristine Beaches

Relax on our sandy beaches and enjoy the crystal-clear waters of Yellow Leaf Bay.
